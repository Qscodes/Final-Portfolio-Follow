I am Quintin Schomaker

And I'm a Videographer

My name is Quintin Schomaker and I am currently enrolled in the communication and development master’s program at Ohio University. I am currently a teaching assistant for the Global Leadership Center (GLC), producing marketing content, facilitating courses, and helping organize the GLC agenda. For my undergraduate degree I studied integrated media, global leadership, and worked for Ohio University in its communication and marketing department (UCM). With a background in video production and design and a M.A. in communication and development my goal is to enhance organizations communication campaigns.

I have experience working on a variety of different video projects. I have worked on documentaries, marketing, promotional, and scripted videos. My skills are very versatile within video production since I can shoot, edit, produce, and direct. My organizational, communicational, and creative skills not only make me a positive contributor to a video production team, but any team working towards a collective goal. Along with my video productions skills I have been fortunate enough to gain experiences in leadership and problem solving. The Global Leadership Program has given me opportunities to work with businesses and non-profits, both locally and internationally. I have travelled to Phnom Penh, Cambodia, Leipzig, Germany, and Accra, Ghana to work with students to provide consulting expertise for non-profit and a government agencies. Through these experiences I gained a skill set in cross cultural communication, international collaboration, and creative innovative problem solving. Visual storytelling, cross-cultural communication and problem solving are my passions.

I am driven, passionate, and can adapt rapidly. I want to work in a creative professional environment where I can grow, learn, and create as a storyteller. Please consider me to be a new addition to your team. I look forward to hearing from you for the possibility of an interview.
